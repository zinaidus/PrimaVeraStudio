My name is Vera Bogatyreva. Prima Vera Studio is the creative lab where I teach, learn from and collaborate with young violinists. I was born in Russia. As a musician I belong to a well-known Novosibirsk school of violinists. The tradition of teaching violin in Novosibirsk goes back to such legendary teachers as Turchaninova, Z.
